///                What is Nexgen Redux you might ask?                        ///
///                                                                           ///
///  Nexgen was an idea a vision a dream which took place in the early 2000s. ///
///  It was quite simple, an alternative dashboard that could be used on the  ///
///  original xbox console.                                                   ///
///                                                                           ///
///  A small team of enthusiast & hobbyist was formed & shortly after a       ///
///  working proof of concept of the Nexgen dashboard was released publicly.  ///
///                                                                           ///
///  The project eventually remained dormant for many years as most of the    ///
///  developers moved on to bigger things however the dream never died.       ///
///                                                                           ///
///  In early 2020 HoRnEyDvL returned to the scene and started working on     ///
///  reviving the project with plans to release a new stable update.          ///
///  -----------------------------------------------------------------------  ///
///                                                                           ///
///             Formation of Team Resurgent & Nexgen Redux.                   ///
///                                                                           ///
///  By July 2020 EqUiNoX & Hishimura combined with HoRnEyDvL. Together they  ///
///  formed Team Resurgent. They shared the same vision but the dream had     ///
///  evolved. It was agreed that Nexgen needed a new lease in life a "Redux"  ///
///                                                                           ///
///  Nexgen was to be transformed into Nexgen Redux. A cross platform engine  ///
///  with core functionality. The goal is to give the flexibility & creative  ///
